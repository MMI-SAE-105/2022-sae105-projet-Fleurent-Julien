In recent years, we have seen the world evolve more and more towards technology. But what will change the world?

The Metaverse

On 28 October 2021, Mark Zuckerberg, the boss of Facebook, had an idea for a new world, the metaverse. For him, it is a virtual world to which we have access with all the immersive technologies. We have,, especially virtual reality headsets. It’s a bit of a mixture between the real world and the virtual world. It will happen little by little, first of all people have to be able to create their virtual home. Then they can take their virtual reality headset and then they are in the place of their choice. Well...for the moment it’s not quite there with the avatars which are not realistic enough but we’ll give them time!

The NFT

Non Fungible Token is a token that is not exchangeable and is unique. But the NFT is more like a line of code. It’s a bit like a digital certificate where you have the token of the digital work, nothing too complicated!

100% autonomous driving

For several years, Elon Musk has wanted to make Tesla’s electric cars fully autonomous without a human driver behind the wheel and according to him it will be available by 2023. He has repelled this several times because it was still quite stable from a technological point of view. He absolutely wants to have a maximum level of safety and autonomy. But for the moment in 2022, the level is between 2 and 3 of driving in full autonomy. Updates are regularly made based on the data he has collected. But for the moment it is trying to do its best to make it 100% autonomous.
