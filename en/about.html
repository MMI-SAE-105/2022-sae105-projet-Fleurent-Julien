Glossary

  • Easter Egg :

    A term used in computer science to designate a reference that technically should not appear.

  • Shoot'em Up :

    Or “shmup” is a category of video game where you control a vehicle or a character by destroying a number of enemies with projectiles to stay alive.

  • Proton Therapy :

    Radiotherapy that uses a particular type of radiation: protons.

  • NFT :

    Non-fungible token is a digital file to which a digital certificate of authenticity has been attached.
